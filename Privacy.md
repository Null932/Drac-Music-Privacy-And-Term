# Privacy Policy for Drac Music

**Last Updated:** November 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to Drac Music (collectively referred to as "the Bot" or "our Services"). By adding the Bot to your Discord server, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Message Content
The Bot processes message content in real-time for auto-moderation purposes, including:
- Message text for filtering prohibited content, spam detection, and rule enforcement
- Attachments and links for security scanning
- Message metadata (timestamps, author information, channel information)

**Important:** Message content is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored unless required for moderation logs.

### 1.2 Server and User Information
- Server IDs, names, and configuration settings
- User IDs and usernames for moderation tracking
- Role information and permission levels
- Moderation action history (warnings, mutes, kicks, bans)

### 1.3 Configuration Data
- Custom filter lists and moderation rules
- Auto-moderation settings and thresholds
- Channel and role configurations
- Server-specific preferences

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for:
- **Auto-Moderation**: Detecting and removing rule-violating content
- **Spam Prevention**: Identifying and mitigating spam patterns
- **Security**: Protecting against raids, malicious links, and harmful content
- **Logging**: Maintaining audit trails for moderation actions
- **Service Improvement**: Analyzing patterns to improve detection accuracy

## 3. Data Storage and Retention

- **Message Content**: Processed in real-time; not permanently stored except in moderation logs when action is taken
- **Moderation Logs**: Retained for up to 90 days or as configured by server administrators
- **Configuration Data**: Stored while the Bot is active in your server
- **User Warning History**: Retained for moderation tracking purposes

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your data to third parties. Data may only be disclosed:
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect the rights, property, or safety of our services
- With your explicit consent

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 6. Your Rights

Server administrators have the right to:
- Configure what data is logged and retained
- Request deletion of server data by removing the Bot
- Access moderation logs through Bot commands
- Modify data retention settings

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 8. Third-Party Services

## 9. Data Deletion
